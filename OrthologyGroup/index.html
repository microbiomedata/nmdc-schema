Class: orthology group

A set of genes or gene products in which all members are orthologous

URI: nmdc:OrthologyGroup

  • KEGG.ORTHOLOGY
  • EGGNOG
  • PFAM

  • SUPFAM
  • CATH
  • PANTHER.FAMILY

Parents
